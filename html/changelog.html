24 December 2020

Arkatos updated:

  • Changelings now use a radial menu to select DNAs.
  • Fixed a case where changelings could extract DNA they already had stored.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Botanists get access to the uplink traitor reagent gun.
  • Uplink traitor reagent gun for CMO/Chemist/Botanist now has piercing darts.
  • All syringe guns can no longer chamber bluespace syringes.
  • Rapid fire syringe guns are now locked behind illegal tech.

coiax updated:

  • Regal rats no longer see themselves as an interloper on examine.
  • Regal rats no longer think they are consuming themselves when eating cheese.
  • Psychologists are no longer mistakenly told they have headaches when near the supermatter.
  • Cursed ice hiking boots dropped by demonic frost miners only become unremovable when worn.

21 December 2020

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Hulks can now throw monkeys/people with tails around by their tails! To do this, get your victim in at least a neck grab, enable throw mode, then click in the direction you want to throw. So long!
  • Carbons will now take damage when thrown at high speeds into things like windows, girders, and other solid objects, in addition to just walls and other people.

New Zealand updated:

  • Added Waitangi Day and ANZAC day holidays

dragomagol updated:

  • xenomorphs, monkeys, dogs, and parrots' strip menus are updated to be like humans'
  • humans now have matching remove handcuff/legcuff buttons in strip menu
  • parrots' inventory will now reflect headsets worn immediately after adding/removing them

28 November 2020

zxaber updated:

  • Cyborgs synced to a Malf AI that activates Doomsday will now have their lamps forced to a red color and enabled to at least light level one. Stealth is no longer an option. Lamp options return to normal upon Doomsday deactivation, no matter the reason, or if the borg somehow loses sync with the AI.
